# Swing Trading: The Strategic Middle Ground

If Day Trading is a "Sprint" and Position Trading is a "Marathon," then **Swing Trading** is the "10K Run." It is arguably the most popular strategy for retail investors on the **Colombo Stock Exchange (CSE)** because it offers a balance between profit potential and time commitment. 

Swing traders look to capture "swings" in stock prices that last anywhere from **3 to 15 trading days**. This allows you to benefit from short-term momentum without needing to sit in front of a computer screen for six hours a day.

---

## The Logic of the "Swing"

Swing trading relies on the fact that prices rarely move in a straight line. Instead, they move in waves. A swing trader's goal is to buy at the bottom of a "Value Wave" and sell at the top before the price pulls back.



### 1. Technical Analysis (The Entry Signal)
Swing traders use indicators to "time" their entries.
* **Moving Averages (20-day & 50-day):** When a stock price stays above these lines, the trend is healthy.
* **Relative Strength Index (RSI):** Traders look for the RSI to drop below 30 (Oversold) to find a "Dip" worth buying.

### 2. Fundamental Catalyst (The "Why")
Unlike day traders, swing traders often look for a reason for the move. This could be an upcoming earnings announcement, a new government infrastructure project, or a recovery in tourism numbers.

## Practical Comparison: Finding Your Tempo

How does Swing Trading compare to the two extremes we've already covered?

| Feature | Day Trading (Sprint) | Swing Trading (Middle Ground) | Position Trading (Marathon) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Holding Period** | Minutes to Hours. | **3 to 30 Days.** | Months to Years. |
| **Time Needed** | Full-time (5+ hrs/day). | **Part-time (30 mins/day).** | Occasional (1 hr/week). |
| **Transaction Costs**| Very High (Daily fees). | **Low to Moderate.** | Very Low. |
| **Overnight Risk** | None. | **Yes (Gaps in price).** | Yes (Long-term cycles). |
| **Main Goal** | Daily Income. | **Growth & Momentum.** | Wealth Preservation. |



---

## The Swing Trader's Workflow
1. **The Evening Scan:** Spend 20 minutes after the CSE closes at 2:30 PM looking at the **AI Stock Engine** for stocks with high confidence scores.
2. **The Set-and-Forget:** Place your "Limit Orders" and "Stop-Losses" in the morning.
3. **The Harvest:** Once the stock hits your target price (usually 5% to 10% gain), you sell and move your capital to the next opportunity.

---

> **Note for CSE Investors:** In the 2026 Sri Lankan market, swing trading is particularly effective in "Cyclical" sectors. For example, following the recovery from **Cyclone Ditwah**, construction and infrastructure stocks (like AEL) often show 10-day "swings" as new contracts are announced. Additionally, because the CSE uses a **T+2 settlement**, swing trading allows you to "rotate" your money efficiently—by the time you sell your first swing, your cash from the previous trade is already settled and ready for the next one.
