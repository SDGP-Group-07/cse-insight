# Market Intelligence Terminal

To navigate the **Colombo Stock Exchange (CSE)** with precision, you need tools that go beyond simple price tracking. The **AI Stock Engine** is a high-performance terminal that uses machine learning to forecast next-day price movements while explaining the "why" behind every prediction.

While traditional charts tell you where a stock has been, this engine uses **Explainable AI (XAI)** to show you where it is likely going and which technical factors are driving that move.

---

## The AI Logic Feed

The core of the engine is the **Prediction Logic Feed**. Instead of a "black box" guess, the AI acts as a digital analyst, breaking down its observations into three clear parts for every signal.

### 1. The Observation
The AI scans technical indicators like **Volume**, **Moving Averages (20-day & 50-day)**, and **Relative Strength (RSI)**. It identifies patterns that humans might miss, such as a drop in trading volume compared to a 5-day average or a lack of price momentum over a 10-day period.

### 2. Why It Matters
This section explains the financial significance of the observation. For example, if the price is trading below the 50-day average, it indicates that the long-term trend is weakening, which may lead to further declines unless a "support level" is found.

### 3. The Pro-Tip
Every observation comes with an actionable tip. These are triggers for you to watch. If the AI detects low volume, it will advise you to "Wait for a breakout" rather than jumping in while the market is quiet.

## Understanding the "Signal Strength"

The engine provides several visual cues to help you judge the reliability of a forecast:

* **The Sentiment Tug-of-War:** This bar shows the balance of power. When the split is near 50/50, it indicates a "neutral" market where neither bulls nor bears have the upper hand.
* **The Confidence Level:** This is the AI's "Truth" gauge. A low score (under 40%) means the current market patterns are messy, and the AI is advising you that the risk of a surprise move is higher than usual.
* **Projected Range:** The AI generates a "Projected Candle" for tomorrow. This shows the **High** and **Low** it expects the stock to hit, helping you set your exit points before the market even opens.
