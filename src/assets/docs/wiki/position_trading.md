# Position Trading: The Marathon Approach

If Day Trading is a "Sprint," then **Position Trading** is the "Marathon." It is the polar opposite of day trading in every way—from the time you spend looking at the screen to the logic used to pick a stock. While day traders try to profit from a few minutes of price movement, position traders hold their stocks for weeks, months, or even years.

This strategy is built for the patient investor who wants to capture "Big Trends" and "Structural Shifts" in the economy rather than chasing daily noise.

---

## The Core Logic of a Position Trader

Position trading is about identifying a high-quality "story" and waiting for that story to play out completely.

### 1. Fundamental Focus
Unlike day traders who live and breathe charts, position traders look at the "Health" of the business first. They study **Annual Reports**, **Earnings Growth**, and **Macroeconomic Trends** (like interest rate changes or government infrastructure projects).

### 2. Ignoring the Noise
A position trader doesn't panic if a stock drops 3% in a day. They understand that markets move in "waves." As long as the company is still healthy and the long-term trend is upward, they stay in the trade.

### 3. High Conviction, Low Activity
You might only make **5 to 10 trades in an entire year**. Because you are holding for the long term, you aren't worried about "timing the perfect second"; you are worried about "picking the right winner."

## Practical Comparison: The Sprint (Day) vs. The Marathon (Position)

Understanding the "Tempo" of these two strategies helps you choose the one that fits your personality and lifestyle:

| Feature | Day Trading (The Sprint) | Position Trading (The Marathon) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Time Horizon** | 1 Minute to 5 Hours. | 3 Months to 3+ Years. |
| **Main Tool** | Technical Analysis & RSI. | Fundamental Analysis & P/E Ratios. |
| **Time Required** | 8+ hours a day (Full attention). | 1–2 hours a week (Occasional review). |
| **Stress Level** | High (Every tick matters). | Low (Daily swings are ignored). |
| **Summary** | Profits from **Volatility**. | Profits from **Sustained Growth**. |



---

## Why Position Trading Works in the CSE
* **Lower Transaction Costs:** Since you aren't buying and selling every day, you pay significantly less in brokerage fees and taxes over time.
* **Capturing Dividends:** Because you hold the stock for months, you are almost guaranteed to be on the books for the **Ex-Dividend (XD)** dates, allowing you to collect cash while you wait for the price to rise.
* **Compounding Power:** Position trading allows the "Magic of Compounding" to work. A stock that grows 15% every year for 5 years creates far more wealth than 100 day-trades that barely break even after fees.

---

> **Note for CSE Investors:** In Sri Lanka, position trading is particularly effective in sectors like **Utilities** or **Banking**. These industries don't move 10% in a day, but they often trend upward steadily over 12–18 months as the economy stabilizes. If you have a day job and cannot watch the market during the 9:30 AM to 2:30 PM session, Position Trading is likely the most profitable and stress-free strategy for your lifestyle.
